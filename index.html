    • Mercedes

  • CSV (verbose)
    • Garmin (embeded into VolksWagen, MAN, Skoda, Seat, etc.)

  • OV2 (minimalist)

installation

4 methods:

Quick (on SD Card)
  • download sd_image.zip
  • unzip it
  • copy the files on your SD Card
  • insert the SD Card in your GPS
  • you should manually import the POI (see explanations)
Secure (on SD Card)
  • download sd_image.iso.zip
  • unzip it
  • burn it on a SD Card
  • insert the SD Card in your GPS
  • you should manually import the POI
Official online
  • download all_files.zip
  • unzip it
  • go to the website of your car maker (eg VW) (please, contact me for others links)
  • insert the SD Card in your GPS
  • you should manually import the POI (see explanations)
Official on SD Card
  • download gpx_files.zip
  • unzip it
  • download Garmin POI Loader
  • upload every CSV or GPX as new POIs
  • connect your car to the Internet
  • update your GPS

explanations

VW Discover Media

  • press the NAV button
  • insert the SD Card into the second card connector of your GPS
  • select Configuration
  • select Gérer la mémoire
  • select Mise à jour my POI
  • waiting for Logiciel de mise à jour disponible then press *mise à jour* then press *suivant*
  • while it terminated, press the NAV button
  • select Carte
  • select Afficher les POI
  • select Afficher les catégories
  • scroll down to GATSO and check which POI you want
  • enjoy
